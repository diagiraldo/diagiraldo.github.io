Holi! I am Diana

I'm Diana L. Giraldo Franco, I'm a researcher in neuroimaging with a background in mathematics and data analysis.

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher at Universiteit Antwerpen, working for the Flanders AI program. My research focuses on developing and testing methods to enhance resolution of brain MRI. Before this, I completed a Joint PhD which granted me the titles of Doctor of Engineering at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, and Doctor of Science at Universiteit Antwerpen. Before that, I studied mathemathics, then I started a Master's in theorethical mathematics, but left it after 1.5 years, and switched to a Master's in Biomedical Engineering which put me in this research path.
